I'm Komo Haruna Dauda — a multi-disciplinary media director and security systems expert based in Abuja, Nigeria. I specialize in creative cinematic productions and advanced **smart systems integration (CCTV surveillance, automation, and perimeter security)**.

From heading 1045 Pixels Studios and leading communications at FIDA Nigeria to designing high-end automated security networks, I've spent 7+ years delivering cinematic films and technical infrastructure.

Driven by a passion for both creative storytelling and advanced technology, I bridge the gap between media art and security engineering. My goal is always to deliver precision, reliability, and visual excellence in all creative and technical setups.

My systems engineering expertise covers: designing and installing high-definition IP CCTV networks with secure remote access, programming automated lighting and climate scenes, and engineering high-voltage electric perimeter security with active alarm integrations.
